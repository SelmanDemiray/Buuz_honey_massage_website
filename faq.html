Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mongolian Honey Massage?

Mongolian Honey Massage is a therapeutic practice combining honey and olive oil for skin rejuvenation, improved circulation, and deep relaxation.

What are the benefits of honey in massage therapy?

Honey is a natural humectant that hydrates and nourishes the skin. It also has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, enhancing skin health.

Are there any side effects?

Our massage uses natural ingredients, so side effects are rare. However, if you have sensitive skin, please let your therapist know beforehand.
